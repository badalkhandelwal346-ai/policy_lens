# 🏛️ PolicyLens

> **Understand Policies, Not Just the News.**

PolicyLens is an AI-powered policy education platform designed to simplify complex government policies into easy-to-understand, interactive, and engaging explanations. Instead of reading lengthy legal documents or relying on fragmented news coverage, users can explore policies through real-world examples, AI-generated explanations, timelines, stakeholder analysis, visual diagrams, and impact assessments.

---

## 🚀 Vision

Government policies affect every citizen, yet most people struggle to understand them due to technical language and scattered information.

PolicyLens aims to bridge this gap by making public policies:

- 📖 Easy to understand
- 🤖 AI-powered
- 📊 Interactive
- 🌍 Accessible to everyone
- 🔍 Fact-based and transparent

# 🌟 Why PolicyLens?

Understanding public policy should not require a law degree.

PolicyLens transforms complex government documents into engaging, visual, and AI-assisted learning experiences, helping people understand not just *what* a policy is, but *why* it exists, *who* it affects, and *how* it shapes society.
